About Me

I have lived in Gilbert, Arizona for the past 12 years, but have been working in Tempe as a professional educator. I earned a bachelors degree in Earth Science and Secondary Education from Northern Arizona University in the beautiful Flagstaff, Arizona. Putting my degree to good use, I have spent over a decade teaching indroductory science concepts to eighth grade students in the Tempe elementary school district #3. Some of the concepts I was responsible for teaching include the foundations of chemistry, genetics, and physics.

I have recently decided to seek new adventures and challenges in the world of information technology. More specifically, I would like to gain skills in web and application development. In order to reach these goals, I am currently attending a coding bootcamp sponsored by the University of Arizona. I am excited to reach my goals and pursue my new exciting career in web development.

Some of my hobbies include exploring the great outdoors, cooking delicious foods from all around the world, and listening to a vast variety of music. I am also an avid woodworker and have a passion for designing and building things. I look forward to applying my passion and creativity into my new carreer as a web developer.
